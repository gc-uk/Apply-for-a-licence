Getting updates on your application

We've made it easy to get updates on what's happening with your licence application.

Once you've submitted an application you can check it's progress by selecting it from your submitted applications list in the Application hub.

If we require any additional information or evidence from you while processing your application, you will get an email containing the details of what we need from you and how to provide it.

In most cases additional information can be provided through the Apply for a licence service, you'll get instructions on how to provide any additional information when we contact you.

Help and support

If you need help using this service, you can contact us on 0121 230 6666 and choose option 1.
